# Academic Equivalency Alternatives

This file captures common alternative academic pathways used by the current MER engine.

## Direct academic pathways

- Passed any 3 subjects in GCE O-Level
- Completed 12 years of formal education
- Completed UCC Certificate in Management
- Holds equivalent qualifications
- No formal qualification but 5 or more years of work experience, subject to interview review

## Qualification equivalency examples

- Singapore GCE O-Level: secondary school leaving qualification
- UK GCSE / Cambridge IGCSE: secondary level
- India Class 12 / Canada Grade 12 / US High School Diploma / Australia Year 12: pre-university level
- Malaysia SPM / China Zhongkao / India Class 10: secondary level
- Germany Abitur / French Baccalaureat / Brunei A-Level: pre-university level

## Manual review triggers

- Qualification title does not match current equivalency table
- Years of education are unclear
- Certificate image is unreadable
- Only informal experience is claimed without documented support
