# English Benchmark Alternatives

This file captures the working English equivalency benchmarks for current course screening.

## Accepted baseline equivalents

- IELTS 5.5 or above
- UCC English Placement Test 70 or above
- PTE Academic 55 or above
- TOEFL iBT 46 or above
- Duolingo 95 or above
- IGCSE English First Language grade C / 5 or above
- IGCSE English Second Language grade A / 7 or above
- GCE O-Level English C6 or above
- UCC English Course Level 3

## If benchmark is not met

Mark:

English requirement not met, recommend UCC English Placement Test or English Course pathway.

## Manual review triggers

- Unfamiliar English qualification
- Grade format is unclear
- Screenshot does not show full score
- Test validity is uncertain
